Questions

Is this macOS only?

Yes. Stonespace Browser is built for macOS 13 (Ventura) and later. No Windows or Linux version at this time.

Can I use it on multiple computers?

Each license is locked to one computer. If you get a new machine, deactivate from the old one first, then activate on the new one.

I get a warning about "Apple cannot verify this app"

Right-click the app and choose "Open" the first time. macOS will remember your choice and open it normally after that.

Do I need internet to use it?

The browser needs internet to load websites, obviously. The license check happens once a day and works offline for up to 14 days.
